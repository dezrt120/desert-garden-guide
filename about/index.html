About Desert Garden Guide

Your go-to resource for desert gardening

Welcome to Desert Garden Guide — your go-to resource for mastering desert gardening. We provide tips, tools, and inspiration for thriving gardens in arid climates.

Whether you're working with a dry backyard in Phoenix, a high-desert plot in New Mexico, or anything in between, this site is built around the actual rhythms of desert gardening: two planting seasons, extreme heat management, water-wise techniques, and the surprising abundance you can coax from native and adapted plants when you learn to work with the land.

From cacti and succulents to wildflowers and drought-tolerant trees, we cover the plants, techniques, and timing that actually work in arid climates — without the generic advice that was written for somewhere with more rain.
